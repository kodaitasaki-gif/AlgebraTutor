Let's learn how to solve algebraic equations step by step.

Example: Solve x + 3 = 6

x + 3 = 6
Our goal is to get x by itself on one side.
x + 3 - 3 = 6 - 3
Subtract 3 from both sides to cancel out the +3.
x = 3
Great! We found that x equals 3.

Remember: Whatever you do to one side, you must do to the other side to keep the equation balanced!
